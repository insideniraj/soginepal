About SOGI

Precision-Engineered Glass Hardware with International Quality Standards

Our Story

SOGI (Solution of Glass and Industries) was founded with a vision to revolutionize the glass hardware industry in Nepal. We recognized the need for premium quality, precision-engineered glass hardware that meets international standards.

Starting as a small manufacturing unit, we've grown into a trusted partner for architects, contractors, and developers across the country. Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us the preferred choice for premium glass installations.

Our Mission

To deliver precision-engineered glass hardware solutions that exceed international quality standards, enabling architects and contractors to create stunning, durable glass structures that stand the test of time.

Our Vision

To become the leading manufacturer of premium glass hardware in South Asia, setting new benchmarks for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Why Choose SOGI?

We combine international quality standards with precision manufacturing excellence to deliver the finest glass hardware solutions.
